# 📈 Amazon Stock Analysis Dashboard

## 📌 Project Overview

The **Amazon Stock Analysis Dashboard** is an interactive **Microsoft Excel** dashboard designed to analyze Amazon's historical stock market performance. It provides valuable insights into stock price movements, trading volume, daily trends, monthly and yearly performance through dynamic charts, KPI cards, and interactive slicers, enabling users to make data-driven investment analysis.

---

# 🎯 Objective

* Monitor Amazon stock price performance over time.
* Compare Open, High, Low, and Close prices.
* Analyze daily trading volume trends.
* Identify monthly and yearly stock price patterns.
* Evaluate stock performance using key metrics.
* Enable interactive analysis through dynamic filters.

---

# 📊 Dashboard Features

* Interactive Day, Month & Year Slicers
* Dynamic KPI Cards for Average Open, High, Low & Close Prices
* Open vs High Price Comparison
* High vs Low Price Analysis
* Trading Volume Analysis
* Monthly Average Adjusted Close Price
* Yearly Adjusted Close Price Trend
* Day-wise Value Distribution
* Interactive Charts & Visualizations

---

# 📈 Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)

* 📌 Average Open Price
* 📌 Average High Price
* 📌 Average Low Price
* 📌 Average Close Price
* 📊 Total Trading Volume
* 📅 Monthly Average Adjusted Close Price
* 📈 Yearly Stock Performance
